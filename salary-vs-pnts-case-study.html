NHL Salary vs Wins

Project Snapshot

NHL Salary vs Wins is an NHL analytics project that evaluates how team payroll levels relate to wins and performance outcomes, then translates findings into executive decision support.

Current evidence base:

  • 19 total artifacts
  • 12 DOCX, 5 PPTX, 2 XLSX
  • Coverage across 3 lifecycle stages

Stage distribution:

  • Define and Frame: 6
  • Analyze and Model: 6
  • Communicate and Decide: 7

The Challenge

Sports organizations often spend heavily on player salaries without a clear, shared understanding of how payroll translates into wins and competitive results.

The project challenge is to connect salary context, statistical analysis, and strategic communication into one coherent decision-support narrative.

The Goal

Build an end-to-end analytical storyline that:

  • quantifies salary-performance relationships,
  • documents assumptions and model logic,
  • supports executive recommendations with traceable evidence.

Approach

  1. Define and frame the problem

    Early artifacts establish research questions, project charter, context, findings framing, and reflection on constraints.

  2. Analyze and model performance dynamics

    Middle artifacts prepare normalized datasets, apply statistical exploration (including correlation and regression), log model-building decisions, and document KPI/strategy alignment.

  3. Communicate and support decisions

    Later artifacts package insights into visual narratives, working papers, decision logs, and executive recommendation briefings.

What the Artifact Set Demonstrates

  • Strong lifecycle continuity from problem framing to executive communication.
  • Practical use of statistical methods alongside communication design.
  • Explicit documentation of model logic and analytical governance.
  • Decision-ready outputs tailored for stakeholder consumption.

Portfolio Value

As a portfolio case, NHL Salary vs Wins demonstrates:

  • business-aware analytics framing,
  • transparent statistical workflow and documentation,
  • conversion of analysis into strategic recommendations,
  • multi-format communication for technical and executive audiences.

Key Takeaway

NHL Salary vs Wins is more than a salary-versus-wins comparison.

It is a structured analytics decision process that links framing, statistical evidence, and executive action guidance in a single artifact chain.

Suggested Presentation Flow

  1. Open with problem and charter artifacts (1 to 3).
  2. Establish findings and project context continuity (4 to 6).
  3. Walk through data and statistical modeling assets (7 to 12).
  4. Cover visualization and working-paper interpretation assets (13 to 17).
  5. Close with executive recommendations and briefing artifacts (18 to 19).
